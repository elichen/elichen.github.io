About

            
This is Karpathy's minGPT (gpt-micro) reimplemented in TensorFlow.js and pre-trained on ~1M characters of Shakespeare. The model was trained offline in Node.js and the weights are loaded for inference here.


            
Architecture

            
    
                
  • 4 transformer layers, 4 attention heads, 128-dim embeddings (~826K params)
    • 
                
  • 128-character context window, 65-character vocabulary
    • 
                
  • Pre-norm decoder-only transformer with causal masking
    • 
            


            
Generation

            
    
                
  • Top-k sampling (k=10) with adjustable temperature
    • 
                
  • Lower temperature = more predictable, higher = more creative
