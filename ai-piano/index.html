How It Works

The Magic

8 buttons control an 88-key piano. You can't play wrong notes—only express direction. Press ascending buttons (1→3→5) and the melody rises. Press descending (7→4→2) and it falls. The AI fills in the musical details.

Contour Preservation

The key insight from Piano Genie: your physical gestures should map to melodic shape. Button order is preserved as pitch order. This creates an intuitive body-to-music connection despite the radical simplification.

Under the Hood

MelodyRNN predicts musically sensible notes. Each button maps to a pitch range, and the AI samples from that range weighted by the RNN's probability distribution. The result: you steer while the neural net handles the music theory.
