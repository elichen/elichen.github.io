    How does it generate?
    3x · y · radius 12mixed nodes 12mixed nodes 4color + ink

    Each CPPN maps a coordinate to pigment and ink. Breeding preserves parent genomes, blends aligned parameters for crossovers, and adds seeded mutations. Flat or unusably noisy offspring are filtered before they hatch.

    Is it learning my taste?

    No preference model is trained. Your selection is the fitness signal for one evolutionary step. The next six images inherit that choice, but the app does not build a profile of you.
