What makes this actual AI?

Nothing is pre-rendered or looked up. This page initializes a multilayer perceptron with random weights, measures its color error, differentiates that error through both hidden layers, and updates the weights in real time. It is small enough to inspect, but it is the same learning loop used by much larger models.
