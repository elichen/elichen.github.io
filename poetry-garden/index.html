How It Works

Drag word seeds directly into the garden to plant them. The AI will generate poetry that grows organically from your planted words.

  • Drag and drop: Drag any word seed into the garden area to plant it
  • Watch poetry grow: AI generates verses that bloom from each planted word
  • Style selection: Choose different poetry forms (Haiku, Free Verse, etc.)
  • Harvest: Click the harvest button on poems to save them
  • Custom words: Create your own word seeds using the input field

Hover over planted seeds for magical word suggestions that create beautiful poetry combinations!
