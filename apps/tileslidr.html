Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?
Simply tap on tiles to slide them into place and solve the puzzle.

How can I use my own photos?
When starting a new puzzle, select the "Use Your Own Photo" option to choose an image from your gallery.

App crashes or issues?
Try restarting the app or reinstalling it. If the issue persists, contact us below.
