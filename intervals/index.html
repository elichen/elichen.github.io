How to Play

Explore musical intervals in three different modes:

  • Identify Interval: Listen to and view the interval, then select the correct name from the dropdown.
  • Create Interval: Click on piano keys to create the requested interval.
  • Ear Training: Listen to an interval and identify it without visual cues.

Use the on-screen controls to play intervals and make your selections. Earn points for correct answers!
