Neural Network Neon

How to Play

Cloud Network: Click on neurons to activate them and watch as the activation spreads randomly through the network!

Feedforward Network: Click on input neurons (left) and watch as the activation propagates through hidden layers to the output (right).

Transformer Decoder: Observe how activations flow through self-attention layers and feedforward networks in a GPT-like architecture.

Use the toggle button to switch between manual and automatic modes.
